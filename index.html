About @Crossroads

Based in Delhi, @Crossroads is an evolving collective of passionate individuals dedicated to fostering creating thinking and meaningful dialogue by organizing a diverse range of academic events. From thought-provoking talks and engaging lectures to dynamic panel discussions and hands-on workshops, this collective brings together experts, scholars, and curious minds to explore ideas and share knowledge.


In collaboration with the Indian International Centre (IIC), New Delhi, @Crossroads aims to create a vibrant forum that bridges disciplines and encourages innovative thinking. By curating events that address contemporary issues and timeless questions, @Crossroads seeks to establish a dynamic platform for intellectual exchange and collaborative exploration.

Intersections @Crossroads

@Crossroads primarily focuses on exploring intersections between academia, industry, public life, arts and culture, and allied fields. At its core, the initiative builds on three overlapping dimensions of intersectionality:


Sciences, Humanities & Social Sciences

Exploring the interplay between the sciences, humanities, and social sciences, and examining how their conversations shape our understanding of diverse contexts, questions, and contemporary challenges.

Disciplines & Perspectives

Bringing together multiple disciplines and perspectives to analyse subjects through comparative geographies, historical time frames, theoretical frameworks, and professional vocations.

Academia & the Public

Strengthening the connection between academia and the public by ensuring that critical themes, ideas, and debates reach a wider audience, including students, researchers, educators, professionals, and engaged citizens.
