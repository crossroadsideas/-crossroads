About @Crossroads

Based in Delhi, @Crossroads is an evolving collective of passionate individuals dedicated to fostering creative thinking and meaningful dialogue through academic events, public conversations, lectures, panel discussions, and workshops.


In collaboration with the India International Centre (IIC), New Delhi, @Crossroads seeks to create a vibrant forum that bridges disciplines and encourages innovative thinking by bringing together scholars, experts, students, professionals, and curious minds.

Intersections @Crossroads
